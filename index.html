About this game

Forest Rescue 2 is an engaging match-3 puzzle game where players help adorable forest creatures defeat pesky beavers that threaten their home.

With vibrant graphics and immersive nature-themed levels, it provides a delightful puzzle-solving experience.

How to play

  • Match-3 Mechanics:

    Swap adjacent pieces to form a line of three or more similar items to clear them from the board.

  • Special Combos:

    Create powerful combinations with four or more items to unlock special boosters.

  • Objectives:

    Complete various objectives like clearing obstacles or collecting specific items to progress through levels.

  • Limited Moves:

    Limited Moves: Solve puzzles within a set number of moves to achieve high scores and earn rewards.

  • Environmental Challenges:

    Overcome unique environmental challenges that add complexity to the puzzles.
